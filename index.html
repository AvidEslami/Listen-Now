Services

At Listen Now, we believe that a hearing evaluation is all about you: your hearing loss, your needs and preferences and of course, your budget. We provide expert, comprehensive evaluation of hearing loss.

Hearing healthcare is truly a personal business and what really matters to us is working with you individually to achieve the best hearing outcome possible. Come meet us and tell us about yourself. It’s the first step towards better hearing.

  • Specialized medical professionals in the treatment of hearing and balance testing
  • Wide range of hearing aid products and accessories from all major manufacturers
  • Full service and setups in Chinese (Cantonese and Mandarin)
  • Open in the evenings from Monday – Friday till 6pm and on Saturdays 12pm-3pm
  • ADP and Insurance plan coverage
  • Exceptional service, and extensive follow-up

Our clinic specializes in the following tests:
  • Complete hearing assessments
  • Hearing aid evaluation
  • Hearing aid fitting, follow up, clean and check
  • Hearing aid battery and accessory sales

    About

    Benjamin Chan, MA. M.Cl.Sc. Reg CASLPO

    Benjamin Chan has many years of experience working in healthcare. He completed his Master’s degree in Science, specializing in Audiology at Western University. He has valued experience in diagnostics, prescribing and fitting hearing aids and assistive listening devices and counselling people with hearing loss and specially trained audiologist in vestibular function. Benjamin Chan invented a new method of taking ear mold impressions digitally and has been registered with the U.S patent and Trademark as of 2013 known as Digimold.
